Build the Financial Statements

An MBA accounting mini-game (Income Statement, Balance Sheet, Cash Flow).

Learning Objectives

  • Identify the primary financial statement impacted by common transactions.
  • Connect transactions to line items on the Income Statement, Balance Sheet, and Cash Flow Statement.
  • Learn through immediate feedback and explanations.
Game rule: Some transactions affect multiple statements. For this game, pick the primary statement the transaction is best associated with for learning purposes.
